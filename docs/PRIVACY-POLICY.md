## 2. Information We Collect

## 4. AI Processing Disclosure

**Important:** We use artificial intelligence to generate draft responses to your reviews.

- **AI Provider:** Google Gemini
- **What is sent:** Review text and dealership context
- **What is returned:** AI-generated response drafts
- **Human review:** All AI responses require your approval before posting

Review text is transmitted to Google's Gemini AI service for processing. Google's use of this data is governed by [Google's AI Terms of Service](https://ai.google.dev/terms).
